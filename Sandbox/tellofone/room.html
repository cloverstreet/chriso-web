Notes
The room is peer-to-peer: audio between you and any other person flows directly, not through a server. PeerJS's public broker handles only the initial handshake.

The room exists while its host is online. If the host disconnects, everyone gets dropped.

Per-peer gain: each meter's blue thumb is the level of that person in your ears. Drag up/down. Doesn't affect what others hear.

Mute Self kills your outbound mic for everyone. Other people's faders only affect what you hear.
