What this is
A small audio room. 3-5 people, peer-to-peer, no install, no signup. All audio processing is off — what you say is what they hear, mic-true.

Bring headphones. Plug into ethernet, or a strong wifi-6/7. Use a real mic if you have one.

Latency is honest browser-WebRTC quality — better than Zoom, not as tight as a Jamulus rehearsal session. Conversational ensemble works. Tight rhythmic ensemble does not (yet).

The room is alive only when its host is online.
