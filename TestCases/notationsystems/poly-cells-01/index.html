Polymetric Looping Cells

Pitch mapping. Choose a tuning system:

Notation test case 02 — cells of different lengths loop on one shared clock and phase against each other. Pitch comes from a selectable tuning (see Pitch mapping); default is 12-tone equal temperament.

Cell header: click the green/red name to mute/unmute. −/+ by the length shorten the cell or extend it with a rest. 🎲 re-rolls that cell's pitches (Undo reverts). shift rotates the notation by the chosen unit; phase slides the playhead ±100% of a quarter.
Edit notes: click to select; click again toggles note↔rest. Drag or ↑/↓ = pitch (scale degree); ←/→ reorders. Numbers 2/3/4/5/6 = 16th/8th/quarter/half/whole, 0 = rest; Delete removes.
Dynamics: select a note, then click a dynamic on the left rail — it sits under the note and holds until the next marking (wrapping around the loop). × clears it.
Cells: + / − cell at the bottom add/remove cells. Presets: shift-click a box to save (notes + offsets + dynamics, not tuning); click to recall. Banks store the tuning too; built-in factory ET ramps simple → complex.
