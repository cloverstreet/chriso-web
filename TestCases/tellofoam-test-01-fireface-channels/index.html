How to use
1. Click Grant Mic Access to expose device labels.
2. Pick your Fireface from the dropdown.
3. Click Open Stream. Note: Chrome caps audio input at 2 channels regardless of device. To get inputs 9-12 (or wherever your mics are) into the browser, route them in TotalMix FX → "Software Playback 1-2" so they appear as the first 2 channels.
4. The rack to the right shows one strip per channel: meter + gain in dB + ARM button.
5. ARMing a channel routes it through its gain into the master monitor sum. Output Routing picks where that sum goes — pick your output device, then which physical channel pair (output side has no 2-ch cap).
6. Base + Output latency is the browser audio pipeline floor. Total tellofoam latency = this ×2 + WebRTC jitter buffer + network RTT.
