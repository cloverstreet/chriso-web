Polymetric Looping Cells

notation test case 02 — three cells of different length and meter looping on one shared tempo clock, so they phase against each other and realign only after their common multiple. Pitch is a 7-tone pélog played at exact frequencies (microtonal, via Web Audio). Independent per-cell tempo is the planned next step.

Cells: A = 4 bars of 4/4 (16 beats) · B = 2 bars of 5/4 (10 beats) · C = 3 bars of 3/4 (9 beats). Common multiple LCM(16,10,9) = 720 beats before all three realign at their starts.
Tuning — . Staff position is an indicative scaffold only (pélog isn't diatonic); the number on each note is the scale degree, and the pitch you hear is the exact cents value below. Swap the cents array in lib/tunings.js to try another gamelan.
